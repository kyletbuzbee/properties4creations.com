Our Story

Founded in 2024, Properties 4 Creations emerged from a fundamental belief that veterans deserve fair treatment in the housing market. For too long, property transactions have been shrouded in complexity and hidden fees, leaving many veterans at a disadvantage.

Our founder, a veteran himself, experienced firsthand the challenges of the housing market after returning from service. Real estate agents pushing overpriced listings, hidden commissions taking 6% of the sale price, and a lack of transparency in property values became the catalyst for creating an alternative.

Today, we operate as a direct-to-consumer real estate company, cutting out the middleman and returning 100% of the fair market value back to property owners. No commissions, no hidden fees, no pressure tactics - just honest, transparent business practices.
