The Properties 4 Creations Standard

Every home we renovate undergoes a rigorous inspection and upgrade process. We prioritize systems that ensure safety, lower utility bills, and long-term comfort.

  • ✓ HVAC: Serviced or New 14+ SEER Units
  • ✓ Plumbing: Leak-free PEX/Copper updates
  • ✓ Electrical: GFCI protection & modern panels
  • ✓ Finish: Luxury Vinyl Plank & Quartz/Granite

Inspection Guarantee

Our properties are pre-inspected to pass Section 8 HQS (Housing Quality Standards) on the first visit. We fix issues before you ever see them.
